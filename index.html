Our Legacy

A century of innovation and excellence

1916

Bayerische Motoren Werke Founded

BMW began as an aircraft engine manufacturer in Munich, Germany

1923

First Motorcycle - R32

BMW produced its first motorcycle, establishing the boxer engine tradition

1928

Automobile Production Begins

Acquired Eisenach plant and began producing automobiles

1936

BMW 328 Launched

The legendary sports car that dominated pre-war racing

1972

BMW M Division Established

M GmbH created to develop high-performance vehicles

1977

BMW 7 Series Introduced

The flagship luxury sedan that defined the modern BMW

1994

Rover Group Acquisition

Expanded portfolio with Land Rover and MINI brands

2013

BMW i Subbrand Launched

Introducing electric vehicles with i3 and i8

2020

Electric Vehicle Push

Launched iX3 and announced ambitious electrification plans

2023

100 Years of M

Celebrated a century of M performance vehicles
